"Business Intelligence (BI) is a technology-drive process for analyzing data and presenting actionable information to help corporate executives, business managers and other end users make more informed business decisions." 

Benefits of using Business Intelligence include:

1) they help to make better and quicker decisions
2) they optimize internal business processes
3) they increase efficienchy from the operational point of view
4) they help companies to get competitive advantages in the market.

Business Intelligence can include several techniques such as:
1) data mining
2) predictive analytics
3) text mining
4) statistical analysis
5) big data analytics
